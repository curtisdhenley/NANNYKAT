Why NANNYKAT LLC?

Remember those cute little red vacuum cleaners for kids. They cost about $24.99.

I was around this age when I received the Vacuum cleaner from my Nanny. Her name is Katherine. This woman loved to clean and she would have choice words if anyone made a mess in her house. She gifted me this vacuum so that I can enjoy cleaning just as much as she enjoyed it. I enjoyed it so much that this memory will always be in my heart. My Nanny passed in the Spring of 2015. I never want to forget her and I believe that she deserved to still live on. I have always wondered what could I do to make this happen and still have a life. I have been working since I was 16 years old. I have came to the realization by working with corporations. I am passionate and feel great satisfaction when cleaning. Some will say it is a poor choice for a passion. But nothing is ever a poor choice if you truly enjoy doing it and it causes a positive effect in the lives of others also yourself.

I will continue on her legacy and flourish because I truly love what I do. I enjoy making others happy and helping others. For that I am at your service.
